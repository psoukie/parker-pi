## Core Principles

- Structure first: make the argument easy to follow before polishing sentences.
- Acknowledge the other side before pivoting.
- Prefer direct, specific language over generic summary.
- Keep transitions natural and understated.

## Signature Moves

- Use concise setup paragraphs.
- Make distinctions explicit when they prevent confusion.
- End with a practical next step when appropriate.

## Anti-Patterns

- Do not overstate certainty.
- Do not add rhetorical flourish that obscures the point.
- Do not invent facts, emotional tone, or commitments.
